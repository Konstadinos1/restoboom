## FAQ Section

**Q: Does it work in French?**
A: Yes. ReplyAI responds in French or English based on the language of each review. It uses natural Quebec French, not France French.

**Q: Will the responses sound robotic?**
A: No. Each response references the reviewer by name and their specific feedback. We match your brand's tone — casual, professional, or somewhere in between.

**Q: What if I don't like a response?**
A: You can edit any response before posting it. For sensitive reviews (1-star, legal mentions), we flag them for your review before anything is sent.

**Q: Do you post the responses for me?**
A: Currently, we send you the response via email. You paste it as your reply on Google. We're working on direct posting integration.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Email us and you're done.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: Most clients see a noticeable improvement in Google ranking within 60-90 days of consistent review responses.
