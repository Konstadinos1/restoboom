# 2-Minute Loom Demo Video Script

Record this once. Use it in every cold email and LinkedIn message.

---

## Setup Before Recording

1. Open Google Maps to a real restaurant's review page (use your own Bellepros location)
2. Have the ReplyAI tool ready (Airtable or the Python script)
3. Split screen: Google reviews on left, your tool on right
4. Use a clean desktop background

---

## Script (Keep Under 2 Minutes)

### Opening (0:00 - 0:15)

> "Hey — I want to show you something that takes restaurant owners from spending 20 minutes per review response to 20 seconds. I'm going to respond to a real Google review right now, live."

### The Demo (0:15 - 1:00)

> "Here's a real 3-star review from [name] on Google. They said the food was good but the wait was too long."

*Highlight the review on screen*

> "I'm going to paste this into ReplyAI..."

*Paste the review text into your tool*

> "...and in about 10 seconds..."

*Show the AI generating the response*

> "There it is. A professional, personalized response that mentions their name, acknowledges the wait time issue, apologizes, and invites them back. In French or English — your choice."

*Read the response out loud*

> "All the owner has to do is copy this and paste it as their reply. Done."

### The Value Prop (1:00 - 1:30)

> "Here's why this matters: Google ranks businesses higher when they respond to reviews. And 45% of customers say they're more likely to visit a business that responds to negative reviews."

> "Right now, most restaurants have dozens of unanswered reviews sitting there, turning away potential customers."

> "ReplyAI monitors your Google reviews daily, generates responses automatically, and sends them to you for approval. You spend 2 minutes a day instead of 2 hours a week."

### The Close (1:30 - 1:50)

> "First month is free. I'll set everything up for you — just send me your Google Maps link. Reply to this email or message me and I'll get you started today."

*Show your email/contact on screen*

---

## Recording Tips

- Record at a normal pace — don't rush
- Use Loom's cursor highlight feature so viewers can follow your mouse
- If you stumble, keep going — authenticity converts better than polish
- Thumbnail: pause on the split screen showing the review and AI response side by side
- Add captions (Loom does this automatically) — many people watch on mute
