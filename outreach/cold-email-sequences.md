## Personalization Variables

Before sending each sequence, populate these fields:

| Variable | Source | How to Find |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| `BUSINESS_NAME` | Google Maps | Search the business |
| `OWNER_FIRST_NAME` | LinkedIn / Google | Owner's public profile |
| `UNANSWERED_COUNT` | Google Maps | Count reviews without replies |
| `RECENT_REVIEWER_NAME` | Google Maps | Most recent reviewer |
| `AI_GENERATED_SAMPLE_RESPONSE` | Your ReplyAI tool | Generate a real sample |
| `MONTHLY_REVIEWS` | Google Maps | Estimate from recent activity |
| `NEGATIVE_REVIEWER` | Google Maps | Most recent low-star review |
| `DAYS_UNANSWERED` | Google Maps | Days since the review was posted |

---

## Sending Best Practices

- Send between 9-11 AM EST Tuesday-Thursday
- Personalize at least the first 2 lines of every email
- Include the actual AI-generated sample in Email 2 — this is the highest-converting email
- For French-speaking owners, send in French. Check their Google listing language.
- Stop the sequence immediately once they reply (positive or negative)
- Track opens and clicks — if Email 1 gets opened 3+ times without reply, send Email 2 early
